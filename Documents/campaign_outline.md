# Elements TD — Campaign Outline

## Structure Overview

The campaign takes place across 5 regions of the realm of Aurelune, each ruled by a corrupted elemental kingdom. The player progressively reclaims each kingdom, culminating in the assault on the capital.

Between missions, the player returns to a **walkable hub camp** that evolves visually and narratively as the campaign progresses.

**Total campaign scope:** 5 regions × 4–6 missions + 1 climax mission each = 25–30 total missions (full game).

**MVP scope:** Region 1 (Emberveil) only — 5 missions + hub camp.

---

## Hub Camp — The Warden's Refuge

A recovered island outpost that serves as the player's base between missions. It is the only safe location in the realm at the start of the game.

### Activities
| Location            | Function                                                        |
|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
| War Map             | Select the next mission; view region progress and lore          |
| Forge               | Unlock new towers using research points earned from missions    |
| Passive Shrine      | Spend research points on permanent passive upgrades             |
| Training Yard       | Test towers and combos against training dummies; no gem cost    |
| NPC Quarters        | Speak to camp companions for lore, dialogue, and story updates  |

### Camp Progression
- Camp begins minimal (one ruined outpost)
- Each region cleared unlocks a new wing or NPC in the camp
- Visual state of camp changes to reflect restored hope as regions are reclaimed

## Region 1 — Emberveil (Fire Kingdom)

**Elemental focus:** Fire
**Tone:** Industrial ruin, volcanic fury, demonic forge-cult
**Enemy introduction focus:** Physical damage enemies; armor-heavy Brutes; fire-corrupted variants
**Unique regional mechanic:** Environmental fire hazards — certain map areas have active lava flows that periodically deal damage to units crossing them (including enemies)

**Research unlocks available after completing this region:**
- Arrow Tower Level 2
- Cannon Tower (new tower)
- Fire Orb priority research (increases Fire Orb drop rate by 20%)
- Passive: +10% gem reward from wave completions

### Mission E3 — Ember Gates
**Type:** Portal siege
**Objective:** Survive 8 waves while keeping the Ember Gate objective above 0 HP; then destroy 2 demon portals before they fully open
**Waves:** 8 defense + portal destruction phase
**Enemy types:** Skitterlings, Raiders, Brutes, Bulwarks (new)

**Unique mechanic — Portal Countdown:**
After wave 8, two demon portals activate and begin a 90-second countdown. If the player does not destroy both portals before the timer expires, a powerful Cinderbound Vanguard spawns and cannot be stopped. Portals have 800 HP each and can be damaged by the player and towers.

**Wave breakdown:**

| Wave | Enemies                                   | Notes                            |
|------|-------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| 1    | 6 Raiders + 8 Skitterlings               | Standard opener                  |
| 2    | 2 Brutes + 10 Raiders                    | Armored pressure                 |
| 3    | 2 Bulwarks + 8 Raiders                   | Introduce Bulwark                |
| 4    | 3 Brutes + 12 Skitterlings              | Mixed density                    |
| 5    | 3 Bulwarks + 2 Brutes                    | Heavy armor wave                 |
| 6    | 2 Bulwarks + 3 Brutes + 10 Raiders      | Complex mixed wave               |
| 7    | 4 Bulwarks + 16 Skitterlings             | Armor and swarm                  |
| 8    | 4 Bulwarks + 4 Brutes + 8 Raiders       | Final defense wave               |

**Portal phase:** All remaining enemies from wave 8 continue pushing while portals are active. Player must prioritize portals while towers hold the lane.

**Map features:**
- Single wide lane with two branching portal alcoves off the main path
- Portals are off-lane; player must physically run to them
- 8 tower placement zones
- Environmental: Active lava channels border the map edges; falling in deals 50 damage instantly

**Rewards:** 120 research points, Earth Orb affinity research unlocked, narrative scene

### Mission E4 — Siege of the Ironworks
**Type:** Escort fortification
**Objective:** Defend the Reclaimer Siege Engine as it advances through 3 holdout points
**Waves:** 12 (spread across 3 holdout phases)
**Enemy types:** Skitterlings, Raiders, Brutes, Bulwarks, Siegeborn (new)

**Unique mechanic — Siege Engine:**
The Reclaimer Engine (HP: 500) advances automatically between holdout points once enemies are cleared. It cannot be repaired. If the engine is destroyed, the mission fails. Siegeborn specifically targets the engine as a priority.

**Phase structure:**

| Phase | Holdout Point       | Waves | Unique threat                   |
|-------|---------------------|-------|----------------------------------|
| 1     | The Smelting Road   | 1–4   | Siegeborn introduced on wave 3  |
| 2     | The Foundry Arch    | 5–8   | 2 Siegeborns on wave 6          |
| 3     | The Ironworks Gate  | 9–12  | Siegeborn + Brute wall on wave 11|

**Tower note:** Towers placed at holdout point 1 do NOT transfer to point 2. Players must rebuild at each position. Budget management is critical.

**Rewards:** 130 research points, Crossbow Tower unlocked, Siegeborn field guide added to codex

### Mission E5 — The Hollow Furnace (Climax Mission)
**Type:** Boss holdout
**Objective:** Defend the Furnace Seal for 10 waves, then destroy The Cinderbound
**Waves:** 10 + boss encounter
**Enemy types:** All Emberveil types; Cinderbound (boss)

**Pre-boss wave notes:**

| Wave | Enemies                                          | Notes                           |
|------|--------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| 1–3  | Standard mixes of Raiders, Skitterlings, Brutes | Building pressure               |
| 4–6  | Bulwark and Brute combos; first Siegeborn        | High armor demand               |
| 7–8  | 2 Siegeborns + mixed wave                        | Dangerous engine-damage window  |
| 9    | Miniboss A (Ashen Stalker) + Raiders             | Player personal combat focus    |
| 10   | Miniboss B (Ironbound) + Bulwarks               | Final DPS and resource check    |

**Boss encounter:** After wave 10, The Cinderbound enters. Phase structure detailed in enemy_roster.md.

**Environmental hazards:**
- Active lava geysers erupt periodically in the boss arena (3 fixed positions, 10 second warning indicator before eruption)
- Eruption deals 80 true damage in a 2-unit radius; player must dodge; enemies are not affected (lore: they're fire-resistant)

**Map features:**
- Wide circular arena map
- 10 tower placement zones (arena ring)
- 3 elevated premium spots on the inner ring

**Rewards:** 180 research points, region cleared, Emberveil restored narrative cutscene, Mage Spire unlocked if not already, research tree tier 2 unlocked

## Region 2 — Thalvere (Water Kingdom)

**Elemental focus:** Water
**Tone:** Flooded ruins, drowned keeps, stagnant canals
**Enemy introduction focus:** Fast and evasive enemies; flying units (post-MVP decision point); Wraiths
**Unique regional mechanic:** Flood tides — periodic flood events raise water levels in certain zones, slowing all ground movement by 25% and disabling some tower placement zones temporarily

**Research unlocks after this region:**
- Mage Spire Level 2
- Arc Ray (new tower)
- Water Orb priority research
- Passive: Player dash cooldown reduced by 0.2 sec

**Missions:** 5 missions + climax (detailed design pending vertical slice completion)

## Region 3 — Greymantle (Earth Kingdom)

**Elemental focus:** Earth
**Tone:** Collapsed fortresses, mining roads, armored siege lines
**Enemy introduction focus:** Armored heavy variants; shield-bearing Bulwark elites
**Unique regional mechanic:** Rubble terrain — certain placement zones are blocked by rubble until the player spends gems to clear them mid-mission

**Research unlocks after this region:**
- Seed Core (new tower)
- Earth Orb priority research
- Passive: Tower repair costs reduced by 25%

**Missions:** 5 missions + climax (detailed design pending vertical slice completion)

## Region 4 — Aelcrest (Air Kingdom)

**Elemental focus:** Air
**Tone:** High cliffs, wind bridges, sky fortresses
**Enemy introduction focus:** High magic resist enemies; displaced-movement enemies; potential flying unit introduction
**Unique regional mechanic:** Wind corridors — certain lanes have wind currents that accelerate enemy movement by 40% for the duration of the current

**Research unlocks after this region:**
- Crossbow Tower Level 3 branches
- Air Orb priority research
- Passive: Player Arcane Surge cooldown reduced by 10 sec

**Missions:** 5 missions + climax (detailed design pending vertical slice completion)

---

## Region 5 — Solharrow (Capital / Crownlands)

**Elemental focus:** All four elements; corrupted variants
**Tone:** Fallen splendor, betrayal, emotional climax
**Enemy introduction focus:** All archetypes; elite corrupted variants of previously defeated enemies; Vorath's personal champions
**Unique regional mechanic:** Corruption spread — corrupted zones grow over time unless cleared; spreading zones reduce tower effectiveness by 20% if placed inside them

**Research unlocks after this region:** Final research tier; cosmetic unlocks; post-campaign mode access

**Missions:** 6 missions + final climax boss encounter with Vorath, The Hollow Regent

**Narrative milestone:** Final confrontation with Elany (the corrupted queen); resolution of the central narrative.

## Post-Campaign Modes
These are unlocked after the credits roll and are not part of the main campaign scope:

| Mode               | Description                                                          |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Endless Defense    | Survive as long as possible on a selected map; leaderboard          |
| Daily Challenge    | Fixed-seed daily map with unique modifiers; shared scoring          |
| Rogue-lite Expedition | Chain of missions with temporary blessings and curses; permadeath on expedition |
| Score Attack       | Complete a mission as fast as possible with maximum stars           |
